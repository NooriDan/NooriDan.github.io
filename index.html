01 — About

I'm a student, researcher, and developer based in Canada, working on Electronic Design Automation (EDA) for analog circuits. My work centers on automating the hardest parts of analog design — topology exploration, symbolic analysis, and component sizing.

My research tools — SymXplorer and SpiceXplorer — are open-source Python packages published on PyPI, used by researchers to derive symbolic transfer functions and run SPICE-in-the-loop optimization with Bayesian and evolutionary algorithms.

I'm also interested in combinatorial optimization and scheduling algorithms, and have built practical tools like ShiftScheduler to solve real-world assignment problems.

02 — Projects

Open Source Work


SymXplorer ↗

A symbolic math toolbox for exploring the design space of analog circuits. Derives and analyzes symbolic transfer functions using SymPy, supports pre-defined models for Sallen-Key, Multiple-Feedback, and Differential topologies, and automates topology exploration. Sized, symbolically or with SPICE, for a given frequency response using Bayesian optimization (Ax) or Evolutionary algorithms (Nevergrad). Published on PyPI. Includes a vision for LLM-driven agentic design workflows.

Symbolic Analysis Analog EDA Bayesian Optimization Evolutionary Algorithms Python SymPy PyPI
★ 7 stars ⑂ 2 forks ◎ 240 commits
SpiceXplorer ↗

A Pythonic, OOP-first compilation of circuit optimization algorithms with SPICE simulation-in-the-loop support. Extracted from SymXplorer to focus specifically on optimization-driven analog circuit sizing. Integrates with optimizers such as Ax and Nevergrad, and provides scalable orchestration of design parameters, measurements, and technology constraints via a declarative YAML-based "Optimization Plan".

SPICE-in-the-loop Circuit Sizing ngspice Ax · Nevergrad Python MacAnalog Org
◈ Active development ◎ 65 commits
ShiftScheduler ↗

A scheduling optimization tool that assigns Teaching Assistants to lab and tutorial sections based on their availability and shift preferences. Solves a constrained assignment problem to maximize preference satisfaction while meeting coverage requirements — a practical application of combinatorial optimization.

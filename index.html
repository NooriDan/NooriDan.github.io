Outside the lab, I served as Co-President of the McMaster ECE Society and play on the McMaster Varsity Rugby team.

Open-Source EDAAnalog Design Automation
SpiceXplorer
↗
Framework I created for SPICE-in-the-loop analog circuit sizing. Orchestrates Bayesian (Ax) and evolutionary (Nevergrad) optimizers against ngspice, driven by a declarative YAML "Optimization Plan."
Creator SPICE-in-the-loop Bayesian Opt. Nevergrad Python · ngspice
SymXplorer
↗
Published PyPI package for symbolic analog circuit analysis. Derives transfer functions for Sallen-Key, MFB, and Differential topologies. Subject of two first-author publications. 7 ★ · 2 Forks · 240 commits.
PyPI Package SymPy · lcapy Python

Hardware & RF DesignAnalog / RFIC
Tunable Common-Gate Bandpass TIA — 3.5 GHz
↗
Novel tunable transimpedance bandpass filter targeting 3.5 GHz (5G / Radio-over-Fiber). Used SymXplorer to systematically explore topology candidates from nodal equations, then sized and SPICE-verified the design. Published in Electronics 2026.
